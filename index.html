Who We Are

A movement, not just an NGO

NayePankh Foundation was born from the simple belief that every child deserves a chance to fly. We work at the grassroots level, building trust with communities, supporting children's education, running food drives, and mobilizing passionate volunteers across India.

We don't just provide aid — we build systems of hope that sustain themselves long after we leave.

What We Do

Our Pillars of Change

📚

Education

We partner with schools and local tutors to ensure no child is left behind. Study kits, coaching classes, and scholarships are at the heart of what we do.

Access · Quality · Future
🍛

Food Drives

Hunger cannot wait. Our rapid-response food drives reach underprivileged families, disaster-affected zones, and children in need — every single week.

Nourishment · Dignity · Relief
❤️

Social Welfare

From health camps to awareness drives, we train volunteers to be agents of lasting social change in their own neighborhoods.

Awareness · Health · Growth

Youth Power
